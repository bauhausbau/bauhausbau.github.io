Building Science

Our team is uniquely qualified enabling us to help them meet their objectives including
'Value Engineering' in construction. Focussing on building envelope performance based on education and training (MArchi in Engineering/Applied Science) we help to prevent issues associated with water penetration, air leakage, vapor diffusion and thermal performance learn more

• Building Envelope Consulting
The building envelope can often be one of the most expensive components of the project. It affects numerous other aspects of the building’s performance including energy usage, occupant comfort, durability, maintenance costs and mechanical equipment sizing. Having the right information to make early decisions and manage risk is critical.

• Air Barrier Consulting
Our holistic approach to roofing, cladding, glazing and waterproofing enables us to offer solutions that consider all aspects of the envelope, as well as the interfaces between building systems. We help project teams prevent issues associated with water penetration, air leakage, vapor diffusion and thermal performance across all climate zones of North America

• Passive House Principles Consulting

We create high performance building enclosures that are durable and energy efficient and provide a full range of building science consulting services: Renovations, Renewals and investigation work on existing buildings
