Development Consulting

Our ambitious team is responding to client objectives, desired project outcomes, budget and timeframe with compelling design ideas. Our development options are pragmatic, viable and innovative. Based on site-specific development potential analysis we offer a broad range of services to consult and assist our clients: Securing key steps to move a project forward, municipal government approvals and community support including marketing design ideas and brochures.

Leading Architecture

Our team incorporates time and budget considerations into our planning process and compelling designs. Our design graphics, production and presentation capabilities, and our ability to respond quickly and flexibly throughout the process help ensure successful outcomes for our clients. We are a full-service architectural firm skilled in the art and science of designing unique environments, exceeding client objectives through leading professional expertise

Urban Design, Master Plan

IMPACT by DESIGN: The future of Climate Policy and future generation starts in our cities and communities. Dedicated to environmentally responsible design for over three decades our team leaders won 16 awards in open international architectural & urban design competitions, ranking #1 to #5

Master Planning, Sustainable Urban Development and Development Phasing- to ensure that development of any component does not create undue limitation or detriment to existing conditions or sales values - are key steps to future development. Throughout the process, we work to ensure the project actually addresses the needs it was designed to meet

Our consulting services range from defining a project vision, feasibility studies to construction completion. In most situations, either a subdivision or rezoning approval is needed, along with development guidelines that ensure adherence to the adopted plan and fit within the community context

Building Science

Our team is uniquely qualified enabling us to help them meet their objectives including Value Engineering in construction. We create high performance building enclosures that are durable and energy efficient. Focussing on building envelope performance within the complexity of multiple building systems performance we help to prevent issues associated with water penetration, air leakage, vapor diffusion and optimize thermal performance. Our team provides a full range of Building Science consulting services including renovations, renewals and investigation work on existing buildings:

Building Envelope Consulting: The building envelope can often be one of the most expensive components of the project. It affects numerous other aspects of the building’s performance including energy usage, occupant comfort, durability, maintenance costs and mechanical equipment sizing. Having the right information to make early decisions and manage risks is critical. With a background of Master degree in Architecture/ Engineering Munich University of Applied Science we ensure to stay on top on professional development and newest technology

Air Barrier Consulting: Our holistic approach to roofing, cladding, glazing and waterproofing enables us to offer solutions that consider all aspects of the envelope, as well as the interfaces between building systems

Passive House Consulting: For nearly three decades our team leaders are advocating and applying Passive House principles to voluntarily reduce the building's ecological footprint and increase energy efficiency of their designs. Although it is principally applied to new buildings, it has also been used for refurbishments: Resulting in low energy buildings that require little energy for space heating or cooling. 'Passivhaus' standard was developed through a number of German research projects in 1990
